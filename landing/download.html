01 — Mission
Brand Mission
We believe that behind every line of data stands a living human being. Our mission is to bring warmth back to HR analytics.

DataPeople is more than a platform. It is a philosophy of care. We humanize data, transforming cold spreadsheets into warm stories about people. Every dashboard is a window into the soul of a company. Every chart is a handshake.

We do not analyze employees. We feel them. Through data.

When an HR director opens our product on a Monday morning, they should smile. Not because the numbers are good. But because the interface embraced them. Visually.

"Data without empathy is just numbers. Empathy without data is just feelings. We unite both worlds."
— From the Luna & Bloom strategic session, day three

06 — Design Principles
Design Principles
Our design hugs the user.
①
Rounded corners — everywhere. Minimum border-radius: 16px. For buttons: 12px. For cards: 16px. For sections: 24px. Sharp corners create anxiety. We do not create anxiety.
②
Soft shadows. Only box-shadow with a pink tint: 0 8px 32px rgba(255, 105, 180, 0.15). No harsh black shadows. A shadow should look like a cloud, not a pit.
③
Gradients over flat colors. A flat color is boring. A gradient is a journey. Every button, every header, every accent — a gradient.
④
Breathing room. Generous spacing. Content must not feel "suffocated." If an element feels cramped — add padding. Then add more.
⑤
Animations — smooth. Transition: 0.3s ease. Hover effects are mandatory. A button should respond to hover like a living creature — gently and with joy.

Comparison: right and wrong:

😬 border-radius: 0
Anxious. Cold.
😊 border-radius: 16px
Warm. Correct.
🥰 border-radius: 24px
Perfect. It hugs.

08 — Forbidden Elements
What Must Never Be Done

The DataPeople brand is about warmth, care, and the joy of analytics. The following elements are strictly forbidden in any materials:

🔴
Aggressive Colors

Red, dark blue, black backgrounds

📐
Sharp Corners

border-radius below 12px

😢
Sadness and Negativity

Any imagery of layoffs, stress, or exhaustion

👔
Stock Photos

People in suits, handshakes, office photos

🩶
Corporate Gray

"Corporate gray is the enemy of joy"

🔲
Flat Design

No gradients, no shadows — boring

The core principle: if an element does not make you smile — it does not belong to the DataPeople brand.
